The paper [Emerging climate impact on carbon sinks in a consolidated carbon budget](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-025-09802-5) has been published in *Nature*. The study integrates recent advances in observations and process understanding to improve estimates of global carbon budget components and assess emerging climate impacts on land and ocean carbon sinks.
