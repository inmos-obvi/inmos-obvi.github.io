InMOS project team members presented the following work at the [Ocean Sciences Meeting 2026 in Glasgow, Scotland](https://www.agu.org/ocean-sciences-meeting):

- **Dhruv Bhagtani** and Laure Resplandy - [Interpreting model-observation mismatches in oxygen variability](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1arvV0HTXbGKqXbmyqd1uCp0km2qqDV2C/view?usp=sharing) (🎤Talk)
- **Renjian Li**, Tim DeVries - [Reconciling geographic variations in particle transfer efficiency with seasonal data-assimilated model](https://agu.confex.com/agu/osm26/meetingapp.cgi/Paper/2033487) (🎤Talk)
- **Olivelli, A.**, Landschützer, P., Bushinsky, S., and Burt, D.J. - [From sparse observations to high-resolution maps: A new data-driven approach to investigate the mismatch between observed and modelled ocean deoxygenation trends](https://agu.confex.com/agu/osm26/meetingapp.cgi/Paper/2033487) (🖼️Poster)
- **Burt, D.J.** and Landschützer, P - [Estimating Ocean Heat and Carbon Uptake and Transport from Novel Earth Observation-informed Machine Learning-based Data Products](https://agu.confex.com/agu/osm26/meetingapp.cgi/Paper/2033487) (🖼️Poster)
- **Nabir Mamnun**, Branwen Williams, Rena Mukherjee, Roberta Hansman, Rolf Sonnerup, Francois Primeau, Nikita Tournebise, Tim DeVries and Heather Graven - [A Comprehensive Global Ocean Radiocarbon Dataset (1700–2023) for Climate Applications](https://agu.confex.com/agu/osm26/meetingapp.cgi/Paper/2028018) (🖼️Poster)
- **Paridhi Rustogi**, Laure Resplandy, Seth Bushinsky, Luc Deike - The influence of waves and bubbles on oxygen in the ocean interior (🖼️Poster)
- **Linus Vogt**, Laure Zanna - Predicting ocean oxygen with conditional diffusion models (🖼️Poster)
