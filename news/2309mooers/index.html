Understanding Extreme Precipitation Changes through Unsupervised Machine Learning

Griffin Mooers is lead author in this preprint that leverages Variational Autoencoders on SPCAM5 output to examine the changes in the upper quantiles of precipitation between a control and warmed simulation. They find intense precipitation changes are mostly controlled by spatial shifts in deep convection.
